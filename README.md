# YourMitra

YourMitra helps children become the hero of their own personalized story.

It is a personalized storybook and event experience platform for families, event planners, and guests. A parent uploads a child's photo, chooses a story, and YourMitra turns that child into a story hero across digital, audio, video, and print-ready formats. For events, planners can create a birthday or school experience once, share QR links with guests, and let each family register a child for their own personalized storybook.

## Product Promise

Every child should be able to see themselves inside a story that feels warm, imaginative, and close to home. YourMitra combines original children's stories, culturally rooted themes, AI-assisted character creation, and polished delivery formats so families get more than a generic gift. They get a keepsake built around their child.

## Who It Serves

- **Parents and grandparents** looking for a meaningful personalized gift or bedtime experience.
- **Children** who become the main character in illustrated adventures built around their name, photo, and story variant.
- **Event planners** who want a premium party or school-event offering without manually coordinating every guest story.
- **Guest families** who can register through a simple QR or short link and receive story links through automated messaging.
- **YourMitra operators** who manage stories, events, orders, guests, and fulfillment from admin tools.

## What The Product Does

### Personalized Story Creation

Families browse the story catalog, choose a template, upload a child photo, add the child's details, and preview a personalized version before purchasing. The child's photo is transformed into a story character and placed into illustrated pages with name personalization, narration, and shareable outputs.

### Story Formats

YourMitra supports multiple ways to enjoy the same personalized story:

- **Digital flipbook** for interactive page-turning, reading, and sharing.
- **Audio narration and background music** for guided storytime.
- **PDF output** for download and print workflows.
- **Story video / movie formats** for hands-free viewing and family sharing.
- **Hardcover books** for premium keepsakes and gifting.

### Event Planner Experiences

Event planners can create an event, select a package, configure the story experience, and share QR or short links with guests. Guests register their child, add personalization details, and receive a personalized storybook experience tied to the event.

The event flow supports birthday parties, school events, guest registration tracking, package-based delivery, ceremony messaging, and post-event upsell paths.

### Guest Registration And Ceremony Flow

Guests can register before or during an event through public links. YourMitra tracks registration status, child details, story generation state, delivery type, and ceremony readiness. Planners can trigger or coordinate storybook messaging through WhatsApp/SMS-style workflows.

### Admin And Operations

The product includes operational surfaces for managing users, planners, events, guests, story generation, orders, pricing, coupons, commissions, notifications, and reports. This keeps the personalized experience manageable as it moves from one-off family purchases to larger event-driven delivery.

## Core Customer Journey

1. A family discovers a story.
2. They upload a clear child photo and enter personalization details.
3. YourMitra creates a preview where the child becomes the story hero.
4. The family reviews the preview and selects a product format.
5. Purchased stories are delivered as digital, video, PDF, or print-ready experiences.
6. The story remains available through the customer's library and share links.

## Core Event Journey

1. An event planner creates an event and chooses a story package.
2. YourMitra generates event links and QR codes.
3. Guest families register their children through the event flow.
4. Personalized stories are generated for registered children.
5. The planner tracks guest readiness and ceremony status.
6. Families receive their story links, with optional hardcover or movie upgrades.

## What Makes YourMitra Different

- **Child-first personalization:** the child's photo and name shape the story experience.
- **Original story worlds:** stories are created for children, families, values, festivals, and imagination.
- **Made for India:** pricing, communication, cultural themes, and gifting patterns are designed around Indian families and events.
- **Multiple delivery modes:** one story can become a flipbook, narrated experience, PDF, video, or hardcover.
- **Event-scale personalization:** planners can offer personalized storybooks to many guests without hand-managing every story.
- **Operational depth:** admin, planner, guest, commerce, and fulfillment flows are part of one platform.
